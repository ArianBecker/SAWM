Nongcebo Langa

Co-founder and winemaker extraordinaire, Nongcebo has perfected her skills in the art of crafting wine through the years as winemaker at Delheim. After winning the prestigious Diners Club Young Winemaker of the Year 2024 award, Nongcebo decided to leverage her knowledge of the South African wine industry towards a platform that would allow her to introduce the wonder of wine to all South Africans. A true visionary of the South African wine industry, Nongcebo strives towards diversifying our wine industry and growing local learning and enjoyment of wines.

Darren Maree

Co-founder and relations specialist, Darren had a meteoric rise to the South African wine industry during his studies at Stellenbosch University. Making a name for himself as your “go to man” when it comes to wine, Darren has advocated the demystification of wine and allow all those interested to explore the wonderful offerings of our Industry. With experience in wine sales and wine education, as well as being in the progress of becoming a Cape Wine Master, there are few young individuals in the industry making such big waves.
